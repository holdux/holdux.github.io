Hi!

I’m Camila, a Product Designer with a focus in UX.

I’ve always been impressed by the impact technology had in my life, so I became a designer to help spread this impact to others by creating the best and most user-friendly tech available.

When I’m working, I always make sure my designs are:


Smart

I use all the data I can get to create a well thought experience that is less generic and more focused on the users needs, behavior and pain points.


Realistic

I communicate with developers to create a solution that both looks amazing and can realistically be built.


Delightful

A good design must be a pleasure to interact with. Even small details can translate into a more delightful experience.
